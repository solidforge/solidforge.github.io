Matthew C. Perry

Front-End Web Developer

Matt is currently a student in BYU studying computer science. Some of his technological interests include web design (both frontend and backend), mobile app development, and data science. Through an education at BYU, he's become proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VBA, and SQL.

This last summer and fall, Matt has had the opportunity to intern for Triathlon Batteries, creating their online battery selector tool. Working with both frontend and backend, he programmed the website to query from a database, display on the webpage, and then be saved in a PDF and sent as a quote. During this project, he learned how to use the Vue.js framework combined with PHP.

Other experiences include working at BYU as a web developer for housing. During his time there, Matt wrote Tableau reports on conference housing and made code improvements on the website. The website development experience gave him experience in the ASP.net framework and Visual Basic programming language.

Matt also enjoys playing musical instruments and writing music. Currently he is co-director of the BYU a cappella group Riff-Dish and member of the BYU Symphony Orchestra.
