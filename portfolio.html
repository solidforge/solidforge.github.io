IS 303 Basketball Season

The Object-Oriented Programming project for IS 303. The program plays an entire season of college basketball games assigning each team. In every game, each team is assigned with a random score from 0-100. A team's games alternate between home and away. Home games give a team 5 extra points. If there is a tie, teams go into overtime to gain up to 15 more points until there is no tie.
